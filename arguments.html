Semantic Discourse Extraction

Argument Pipeline

Labeled transcripts are converted into persona-specific training data by batching same-speaker continuations and using prior conversational turns as context (the prompt) and the target speaker's response as the completion. This works well for style transfer and mimicry, but I wanted a reasoning layer underneath it.

So, I mined the transcript corpus using local Qwen3-30B MoE. Across a couple dozen of extractions passes, the system identified recurring beliefs, topic ownership, argument patterns, domain expertise, behavioral tendencies, and long-running positions. These artifacts were then transformed into runtime embeddings for the four Besties.

Argument Chains

who said what, who pushed back, what survived
CHAIN example structure
▼
An argument chain is not a summary of a topic. It is the local machinery of the conversation: who made the claim, who challenged it, who clarified it, who agreed, and which position each speaker ultimately held.
