Semantic Discourse Extraction

Argument Pipeline

Speaker-labeled transcripts are converted into training data by consolidating consecutive segments from the same speaker. Prior conversation becomes the prompt, and the target speaker's response becomes the completion.

Example: Original Transcript

Bob (IT): Do we have the budget to move the database to the cloud?

Luke (Finance): Maybe, but costs could explode.

Luke (Finance): I'd like to understand our long-term storage requirements before making any decisions.

Bob (IT): The ops team spends half their week maintaining servers.

Example: Transcript as Training Data

Bob (IT): Do we have the budget to move the database to the cloud?

Luke (Finance): Maybe, but costs could explode. I'd like to understand our long-term storage requirements before making any decisions.

Bob (IT): The ops team spends half their week maintaining servers.

This works for style transfer for the chatbot personas: The model learns vocabulary, phrasing, cadence, and conversational habits but is suceiptible to drift.

To create an anchor, I mined transcripts using local Qwen3-30B MoE to surface recurring beliefs, topic ownership, argument patterns, domain expertise, behavioral tendencies, and long-running positions.

Argument Chains

who said what, who pushed back, what survived
CHAIN example structure
▼

An argument chain is not a summary of a topic. It is the local machinery of the conversation: who made the claim, who challenged it, who clarified it, who agreed, and which position each speaker ultimately held.

Chamath
claim
Frames a position and attaches a market, product, or incentive explanation to it.
Sacks
rebuttal
Pushes back on the framing and redirects the argument toward institutional incentives or policy consequences.
Friedberg
explanation
Breaks the issue into mechanisms, probabilities, or second-order effects.
JCal
question
Forces the argument back into plain language, audience framing, or practical stakes.
Group
position
The final state records where the speakers landed, where they disagreed, and what remained unresolved.
