Project Background

Motivation

The original goal in 2025 was simple: create a helper iOS app that could rewrite my ideas in the voices of the All-In hosts. I think style transfer is fascinating and, because frankly, I think the idea is hilarious.

Having the voices of the All-In hosts implied having transcripts from their podcast, which had to be created from the ground up. Transcripts required accurate speaker labeling. Accurate speaker labeling required HITL forensic review and repair. So on, so forth.

Every milestone revealed another layer. The princess was always in another castle.

So what began as a "move fast and MVP chatbot" project gradually turned into a serious observability project for AI pipelines. Transcripts have been, by far, the most challenging and rewarding part of the project. The pursuit of quality and ground-truth accuracy led to a series of increasingly specialized tools:

Clownfish — maps speaker IDs to names and extracts labeled training audio and video.
Brody — applies lexical heuristics and transcript inspection workflows to identify, review, and repair transcription and classification errors.
BERD-1 (Bio-Ectoplasmic Resonance Detector) — compares production and experimental Whisper decoding paths to surface Ghost Audio signals — instances where audible speech may vanish during transcription.

At some point the pipeline stopped being a prerequisite for the project and became the project itself. The chatbot was the destination. The transcripts were the journey.

The pipeline detail is in Voice Classification ↗. The edge cases are in HITL Heuristics ↗. What fell out of all of it is in Chasing Ghosts ↗.
