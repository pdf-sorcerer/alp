ALL-LLLM POD

Hey, everybody!

Welcome to the number one ALL-LLLM POD: a technically serious generative AI system built around an intentionally unserious premise: conversational chatbot parodies of the All-In Podcast hosts (aka: Besties).

The Besties are reconstructed from 560+ episodes using two parallel pipelines. The audio pipeline handles VAD, ASR, diarization, speaker classification, and human-in-the-loop transcript repair. The video pipeline extracts non-verbal patterns — posture, movement, gaze, and expression — through face and body recognition.

See the left navbar for the big picture. More detailed looks at helper apps and supporting workflows are in Important Sidequests ↗.
